The global impact of the COVID-19 crisis has been vast, resulting in lost employment opportunities for many industries. To counter the impact to Outfit’s community of hospitality, food and beverage and retail professionals, we’re introducing new features making it easier for Brands to connect with Digital Talent and bring IRL events into the URL. Together we can create new opportunities for Brands to make an authentic connection to real people, and keep valued Talent working.
