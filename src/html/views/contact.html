Frequently Asked Questions
What is Outfit?
Outfit is a digital platform changing the way brands staff their events by handing over control to the users.
Through Outfit, Brands have direct access to our Talent feed, connecting them to a marketplace of qualified event staff, and the ability to hand-select a dream team of people who will make their party.
Talent leverage the platform to highlight their skills, build their personal portfolio and maintain a steady stream of bookings while providing high-quality service for Brands.
How is Outfit different from a standard staffing agency?
Outfit is different because we’re not an agency. We’re a digital platform that hands over control to the users. Talent act as their own agents, while Brands hand-select and communicate directly with their event team. Staffing simplified.
What type of Talent do you provide?
Outfit prides itself on our Talent feed, a marketplace of qualified front-of-house event staff:
Bartenders – Bar Service and Mixology
Servers – Food and Beverage Service
Personalities – Hosting, Check-in, Brand Ambassador
Captains – Event Staff Management
Concierge services are available upon request for personalized solutions including access to additional Talent profiles such as photographers and DJs. If your event requires special support, contact hello@outfittalent.com to discuss additional service requirements.
What is the cost of Talent?
Outfit values complete payment transparency, and by allowing users to manage all details through the platform Brands pay less, Talent take home more. The cost per Talent is $38/hour, inclusive of a 24% connection fee. On average, the cost to Brands is about 30% lower than traditional staffing services and Talent take home 30% more.
How are Talent paid?
Once Brands confirm their Talent, Outfit automatically generates a digital invoice with a breakdown of all costs. Enter card details to secure Talent with payment processed upon completion of the event. Once the Brands confirms the Talent's hours, payment is triggered via our secure payment system, Stripe. You'll then be able to access your invoice and download a PDF.
What types of events does the Outfit platform support?
Events come in all shapes and sizes and Outfit's purpose is to hand over control of the staffing process, allowing Brands to custom-tailor all details to your specific need. Whether your event is a high-impact product launch, limited engagement pop-up experience, or a private party, Outfit’s platform is designed to support any scale.
Is there a minimum number of hours required for booking Talent?
No minimum hours required. Create your event, review Talent applications and book the team best suited to your needs.
Is there a user ratings system?
Yes. Users have the ability to rate each other at the end of every shift, ensuring accountability for delivering high-quality service.
Does Outfit comply with labor and employment laws?
Yes.
How does Outfit screen Talent? Is there a training program?
Talent must apply and agree to our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy in order to use the Outfit platform. Our ratings systems ensures accountability on both sides of the platform for Brands and Talent alike.
As Outfit is not an agency, all Talent on the platform are independent contractors, meaning they are self-employed and representing themselves. By posting an event, Brands create an employment opportunity that the Talent applies for. Brands are able to confirm and hire Talent based on profile, work history, linked social media accounts, ratings, and accountability with Outfit platform.
Do you provide a catering permit or liquor license?
Outfit is primarily a platform connecting event producers directly with Talent so we don't offer catering or liquor licenses. However, we are happy to provide a list of preferred catering partners who can provide licenses as part of our concierge service. Please reach out to hello@outfittalent.com with any questions.
How can talent create engaging profiles that attract brands?
Outfit celebrates Talent and we encourage you to use the platform to build your personal brand. Complete your profile by adding pictures, highlighting your skills and experience, work history, linked social media accounts and ratings history.
How will I be notified of new events and shifts?
Anytime an event is posted in a Talent's area of expertise, you'll receive a notification alerting you to the new opportunity. If you have been specifically invited to a job, you will receive a notification with details. You'll then need to accept or reject the invite.
