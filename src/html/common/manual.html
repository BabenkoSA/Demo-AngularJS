Step 1: Connect Instagram
  • In the Instagram app, go to your account and tap Menu
  • Select Settings , then Account
  • If you are migrating from a personal account, choose Switch to Professional Account and tap Business
  • Select your Category by choosing one that best describes what you do
Step 2: Connect Facebook through Instagram
  • In the Instagram app, go to your main Profile Page and select Edit Profile
  • Under Profile Information, tap Page
  • Select Create Facebook Page (or Connect to an Existing Page if you already have one) and follow instructions
Step 3: Finalize Outfit Live set-up
  • Return to the Digital Talent Application or log into your Outfit Live profile and click Connect Instagram via Facebook
